Prime Detective

Think of a number and it may be a prime number. Don't believe it? Give it a try!
You may have heard that prime numbers are not so common.
This project breaks that myth.

Enter a number with 10 digits or less that does not end in

**** 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 ==> since all such numbers are divisible by 2 and hence cannot be a prime number.

**** 5 ==> since all such numbers are divisible by 5 and hence cannot be a prime number.

Also, numbers whose digits add up to a multiple of 3 are all divisible by 3 and hence cannot be a prime number. Hence avoid them as well.
